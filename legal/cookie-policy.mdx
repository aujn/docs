### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, mobile, or tablet) when you access a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide functionality, and gather usage statistics.

* **First-party cookies** are set directly by DsReplay.
* **Third-party cookies** are set by services we engage, such as analytics or advertising partners.
